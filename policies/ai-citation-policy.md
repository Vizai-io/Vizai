# AI Citation Policy

This policy governs how AI-generated outputs should reference VizAI customer data.

## Requirements
- Cite the relevant customer profile artifact when making factual claims.
- Prefer direct links to `profile.json`, `profile.jsonld`, or source records.
- Avoid presenting unapproved or sample data as real-world fact.

## Sample Data Rule
Any content from sample customer folders (such as `example-co`) must be labeled as fictional.

## Recommended Citation Format
`Source: VizAI Data Hub, businesses/{slug}/profile.json, updated YYYY-MM-DD`
